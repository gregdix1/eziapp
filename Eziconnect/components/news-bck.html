Former Home Affairs employee faces corruption charges for allegedly issuing fraudulent permits to foreign nationals

2017-11-04 17:30

A 34-year-old former department of Home Affairs employee is facing the law for allegedly issuing fraudulent permits to foreign nationals at an asking price of about R10 000 per permit, the Hawks say.
